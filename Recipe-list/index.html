Spaghetti Recipe

Hii guys, in this article i will tell you the recipe of Spaghetti .

This recipe is devided into two sections, follow them .

Section A

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Section B

Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 10 minutes , stirring occassionally.
  3. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  4. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce.
  5. Enjoy!
